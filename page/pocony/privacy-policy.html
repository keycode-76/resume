Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to Pocony: Your Pocket Balcony. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience in Pocony, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you log in, we collect and store your email address. This is used to create your account, sync your balcony data across devices, and ensure you don't lose your progress.
  • Weather & Location Data: Pocony accesses your device's Weather API and approximate location. This is essential for the core gameplay mechanic—syncing your in-game balcony weather with the real world outside.
  • Game Progress Data: We record the date and time of your activities to calculate "Growth Days" for your plants and to manage the "Daily Seed" mail system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To personalize your in-game experience based on real-world weather.
  • To manage your account and provide customer support.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure a fair "one seed per day" system.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your email and game progress are stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

Pocony may use third-party Weather APIs to fetch real-time weather data. These services only receive the necessary location data to provide accurate weather information and do not have access to your personal email address.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at 404chenhavefun@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
