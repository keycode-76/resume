Pocony

Build a little balcony on your phone. Where plants grow with the real weather outside.

When it rains outside, or when the sky clears up, the weather in the game changes too. Watch your unique flowers bloom at their own pace, synced with your real-world environment.

Magical Features

Real-time Weather

The sky in your pocket balcony reflects your real sky. Rain, sun, or clouds—your plants feel it all.

Daily Seeds

Check your mail every day! You get one chance daily to receive unique seeds to grow your collection.

Unique Growth

Each flower has its own function and grows at a different pace. Patience is the key to a beautiful balcony.

How to Start

1

Login with Email

Securely sign in with your email. We'll save your balcony state so you never lose your plants.

2

Sync with Nature

Pocony uses your phone's weather API and time to calculate growth days and environment conditions.

3

Collect & Grow

Plant your 3 starter flowers and expand your balcony as you receive new seeds in the mail.
